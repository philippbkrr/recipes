My favorite recipes

In the following section you will find a collection of my favorite dishes and the recipes to make them.

Arancini

Arancini are a traditional Italian dish made with rice balls, filled with various ingredients, and then deep-fried until golden and crispy.

i personally prefer the version with meat and mozzarella

Lasagna

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish made with lasagna noodles, filled with various ingredients, and then baked until golden and crispy.

i personally prefer the version with beef and parmesan

Spaghetti Carbonara

Spaghetti Carbonara is a traditional Italian dish made with spaghetti, eggs, and parmesan. It is a simple dish that is easy to make and tastes great.

i personally prefer the version with parmesan and eggs (because there is also a version with cream)
